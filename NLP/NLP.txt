5 phases of NLP 
1. Lexical and morphological analytics   - word level breakdown 
2. syntactic analytics  -- Grammer and structure(POS)
3. semantic analytics -- liternal meaning of the word ( person, location, general information - NER  - bank  - riverbank , financial bank )
4. Discourse integeration -- context and how they framed the sentence , integeration of multiple sentences( sam is a male , he, he)
5. pragmatic analytics(sentiment) -- intent meaning & context , how can i help?|what you want ? - hidden intent, emotion, behaviour, direct/indirect
